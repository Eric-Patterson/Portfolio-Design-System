Concept

From the start I always wanted to have my name in a custom made font for my name, that had a modern design to it. I wanted to add emphasis on the E,R, P and A to have missing elements from the letters as well as the font to have slightly curved edges.

Brand Values, Purpose & Objectives

Tone of voice

I will be using a very mellow, calm and direct tone of voice. Very professional as I am interested in my portfolio to been seen by both future clients and potential new employers. Therefor a very business centric language will be used.

Values

I strive to always improve my workflow, to consistently seek innovation whether it be in my design, or new technology stacks.

Objective

The objective of my portfolio is to showcase my work to potential new employers and clients.

Purpose

My purpose is to have a significant business relationship with my future clients and to help execute their dreams into reality.
